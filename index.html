    Email Signature Generator

Creating your own Meyer Sound email signature:


  1. Read the instructions carefully.
  2. On the form, on this page, select which email client you use.
  3. Enter your name in the field provided.
  4. In the "Title" section, please enter your job title as it is displayed on your business card.
  5. Enter your phone number(s) in the "Phone Number(s)" section.
    • Form supports up to 3 phone numbers. Blank inputs are ignored.
    • Please lead with your country code.
    • All punctuation is honored. Use your business card as a point of reference for formatting.
  6. Lastly, enter your Meyer Sound email address in the space provided.
    • All capitalization is automatically converted to lowercase.
  7. When ready, click "submit" to generate email signature.
